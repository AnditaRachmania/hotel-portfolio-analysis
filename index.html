📂 Data Source
Hotel Booking Demand Dataset
This analysis is based on a real-world hotel booking dataset covering 119,390 booking records from two hotel properties in Portugal — a Resort Hotel in the Algarve region and a City Hotel in Lisbon — spanning booking arrivals from July 2015 to August 2017. All personally identifying information has been anonymised.
